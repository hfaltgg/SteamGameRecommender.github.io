About

People are hard to find the games they like on Steam because there is too much software on the platform to make sure each of them. The category and the “guess you like” part on Steam cannot provide certain and useful result in many conditions.

Our website is delicate to provide a recommender system for the steam software. According to the playtime of other users on the Steam, the system will evaluate a rank, and them recommend the games based on the similar users with you.

In this demo, you could choose 3 games on the 10 popular games on Steam you like, our system will assume you are the user who loves the 3 games and return 3 games we recommend to you. There is totally 20 games in the database in the demo website, you could try any combination if you like to view if the recommendation reliable.

This page was made by Tian Xie for CS525 Informational Retrieval & Social Web Project Professor Kyumin Lee Spring 2018 Worcester Polytechnic Institute.
